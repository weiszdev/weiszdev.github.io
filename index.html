The Story Behind the Work

Trauma Bond

Mechanical engineer. Professional athlete. Then Lyme disease, traumatic brain injury, and cancer — all converged. The medical system offered prescriptions. The system offered labels. Neither offered a path forward.

Four years as a single father taught what no degree could: that healing isn't passive. It's engineered. You identify the broken system, you trace it to the root, and you rebuild — one variable at a time.

That's what Trauma Bond is about. Not the clinical definition of trauma bonding — the real one. The bond between what happened to your body, what it did to your nervous system, and who you became because of it. And how faith, neuroscience, and deliberate action break the cycle.
